Research Themes

Catheter & Tool Robotics

Mechanisms, actuation, and control for flexible tools in cardiac and neurovascular interventions.

  • Steerable catheters and guidewires.
  • Force sensing and haptics.
  • Navigation in tortuous anatomy.

Imaging & Guidance

Systems that use fluoroscopy, ultrasound, and MRI to guide robots and clinicians in real time.

  • Image-based tracking and registration.
  • Motion compensation.
  • Human–robot shared control.

Translational Prototypes

End-to-end development from concept to benchtop testing with clinicians and industrial partners.

  • Defined verification and validation pipelines.
  • Bench, phantom, and pre-clinical testing.
