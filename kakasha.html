Research Scholar in Biomedical engineering-

My name is Kahkasha, and I am a pharmacologist with expertise in both wet lab and bioinformatics applications. I received my B. Pharm degree from Dr. Bhim Rao Ambedkar University, Agra (2020), followed by a Master of Pharmacy (specialisation in Pharmacology) from SPER, Jamia Hamdard University, New Delhi (2022). My research skills include implementing experiments in vitro and in vivo, focusing on cardiovascular, neurobehavioral, and osteoarthritis pain. I have hands-on expertise in in vitro, in vivo, molecular biology, and bioinformatics applications. My PhD endeavour cantered on synthesis of MOF-associated drug delivery system targeting cancer. This research initiative aims to develop a precise, controlled-release mechanism for drug loading and release, with the goal of enhancing therapeutic efficacy in cancer treatment.
