Project Research Associate

Dr Pratibha Pansari

Research Associate-

Dr. Pratibha Pansari completed her PhD from Sharda University, with her research executed at the Centre for Nano Science and Engineering (CeNSE), Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Bangalore, under the INUP-i2i programme (MeitY, Govt. of India). Her academic journey and research interests revolve around developing innovative, future-oriented solutions for real-world healthcare problems by integrating material science, chemistry, and biology. Her career objective is to give the useful output of research work, no matter how tiny it is in the house of researchers. She aspires to inspire the next generation to explore beyond the confines of their minds and imagination. She is enthusiastic about science and technology with the added ingredient of human philosophy and her spare time belongs to literature.
